About TKE

Welcome to my IUPUI Tau Kappa Epsilon page. I am a junior at IUPUI and have been a member of TKE since the Fall of 2019. Ever since I joined this fraternity, it has become a major part of my life.

Why join Tau Kappa Epsilon?

There are 6 fraternities on our campus with TKE being one of them. I have never met such a close-knit group of men in my life. This really shows when you see how good the leadership, brotherhood, and involvement is throughout this group.
